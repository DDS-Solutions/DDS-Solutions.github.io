Common Questions

How do I mount my phone?

There are thousands of free STL files on Thingiverse/Printables for "Phone Tripods" or "Phone Mounts" that you can print yourself!

Can I view on my PC?

Yes, we have a browser viewing mode. You can watch your print progress in a tab while you work on your computer.

Is there a monthly fee?

Never. Avoid expensive hardware and subscriptions. For a one-time price of just $1.99, you get the ability to install the app to other Android devices as many times as you need.

Need longer USB cables or AC Battery Charger?

To keep your camera running 24/7, you may need a long power cable or a reliable adapter. Visit our Accessories Page for recommendations.
