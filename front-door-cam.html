Common Questions

Will it work through glass?

Yes! Just make sure to turn off the "Flash" or any IR lights on the phone to avoid reflection glare at night. Standard street lights are usually enough for modern phone sensors.

Is there a monthly fee?

Never. Avoid expensive hardware and subscriptions. For a one-time price of just $1.99, you get unlimited security monitoring forever.

Need longer USB cables or AC Battery Charger?

To keep your camera running 24/7, you may need a long power cable or a reliable adapter. Visit our Accessories Page for recommendations.

Is it waterproof?

Most phones aren't waterproof enough for rain. That's why we recommend placing it inside looking out a window. Safe, dry, and secure.
