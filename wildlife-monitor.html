Common Questions

How close can I focus?

Most smartphones can focus as close as 3-4 inches. This allows for incredible detail if you can position the camera near a feeding spot.

What about battery life?

For long sessions, we recommend plugging the camera device into power. A portable power bank is great for window setups where an outlet isn't close.

Is there a monthly fee?

Never. Avoid expensive hardware and subscriptions. For a one-time price of just $1.99, you get the ability to install the app to other Android devices as many times as you need.
