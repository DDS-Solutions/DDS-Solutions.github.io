Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Remote Camera App. We believe in complete transparency and data ownership.

Overview: This application is designed to function entirely on your local network. It does not connect to the internet, does not use cloud storage, and does not collect any personal data.

1. Camera & Microphone Data

The app uses your device's camera and microphone to stream video and audio. This data is transmitted only to other devices on your local Wi-Fi network that you explicitly connect to.

  • This data is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • This data is not accessible to the developers.

2. Local Storage

If you choose to capture photos or record videos using the app, these files are saved directly to your device's internal local storage.

  • You have full control over these files.
  • We do not have access to view or delete these files.

3. Location Data (Wi-Fi Discovery)

This app does not collect, track, or store your GPS location.

However, the Android operating system requires Location Permissions to discover other devices on a local Wi-Fi network (via Wi-Fi Direct or Network Discovery). We do not use this permission for any other purpose than connecting your devices.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking services (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel). Your usage data remains private on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. As the app is a utility for local monitoring and does not transmit data to external servers, no child data is ever collected by us.
