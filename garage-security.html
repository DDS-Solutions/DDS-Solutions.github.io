Common Questions

Will it survive the cold/heat?

Most phones are quite durable, but extreme temperatures can affect batteries. For permanent garage setups, we recommend removing the battery if the phone supports it, or ensuring the garage isn't below freezing.

What about weak Wi-Fi?

Garages often have weak signals. Our app is optimized for low-latency, but if the signal is too weak, consider a cheap Wi-Fi extender for the garage.

Is there a monthly fee?

Never. Avoid expensive hardware and subscriptions. For a one-time price of just $1.99, you get the ability to install the app to other Android devices as many times as you need.

Need longer USB cables or AC Battery Charger?

To keep your camera running 24/7, you may need a long power cable or a reliable adapter. Visit our Accessories Page for recommendations.
