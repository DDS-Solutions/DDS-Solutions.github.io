Under the Hood

Zero-Config Pairing

Powered by Android's Network Service Discovery (NSD). The app automatically finds devices on your network‚Äîno IP addresses to type, no QR codes to scan. Just open and connect.

Direct Socket Stream

We use a custom-built UDP/TCP Socket Server directly on your device. This eliminates the "middleman" server found in other apps, ensuring data privacy and reducing lag.

Background Service

Utilizing Android's Foreground Service architecture, the camera continues to stream securely even when your screen is off or the app is minimized, saving battery life.

Technical Expectations

  • Minimum Android 12 OS required.
  • USB Power recommended for sustained periods of use.

Use Considerations

  • Performance depends on the number of connected devices/browsers.
  • Wi-Fi/Hotspot bandwidth limitations may affect stream quality.
