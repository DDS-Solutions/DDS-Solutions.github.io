⚠️ Critical Safety Notices

Battery Health

Continuous charging can generate heat. For 24/7 use:

  • Remove the phone case to improve heat dissipation.
  • Reduce screen brightness to the minimum.
  • Consider apps or settings that limit maximum charge to 80%.

Cable & Adapter Safety

Not all cables are rated for continuous current.

  • Discard any cable with fraying or exposed wires immediately.
  • If an adapter feels hot to the touch, replace it with a higher-amperage rated unit.
  • Avoid daisy-chaining extension cords.
